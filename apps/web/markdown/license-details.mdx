# License Details

This document describes the licenses available for images uploaded to OpenFrame. Users can choose one of the following licenses when uploading their work. OpenFrame may display the selected license and attribution information alongside uploaded content.

---

## 1. All Rights Reserved

**Identifier:** `all-rights-reserved`

**Description:**  
The uploader retains full copyright ownership of the image. No one else may copy, modify, distribute, or use the image without the copyright owner's explicit permission.

**Permissions:**

- ❌ Commercial use
- ❌ Modification
- ❌ Redistribution
- ❌ Use without permission

**Requirements:**

- Explicit permission from the copyright owner for any use.

**Best For:**

- Users who want maximum control over their work.

---

## 2. Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 (CC BY 4.0)

**Identifier:** `CC-BY-4.0`

**Official License:** https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

**Description:**  
Others may copy, distribute, modify, and use the image for any purpose, including commercial use, as long as they provide proper attribution to the original creator.

**Permissions:**

- ✅ Commercial use
- ✅ Modification
- ✅ Redistribution

**Requirements:**

- Attribution to the original creator.

**Best For:**

- Users who want their work to be widely reused while receiving credit.

---

## 3. Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-SA 4.0)

**Identifier:** `CC-BY-SA-4.0`

**Official License:** https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/

**Description:**  
Others may copy, modify, and distribute the image, including for commercial purposes, provided they give attribution and license any derivative works under the same license.

**Permissions:**

- ✅ Commercial use
- ✅ Modification
- ✅ Redistribution

**Requirements:**

- Attribution to the original creator.
- Derivative works must use the same license.

**Best For:**

- Users who want derivatives to remain open under the same terms.

---

## 4. Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 (CC BY-NC 4.0)

**Identifier:** `CC-BY-NC-4.0`

**Official License:** https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/4.0/

**Description:**  
Others may copy, modify, and share the image for non-commercial purposes only, with attribution.

**Permissions:**

- ❌ Commercial use
- ✅ Modification
- ✅ Redistribution

**Requirements:**

- Attribution to the original creator.
- No commercial use.

**Best For:**

- Users who want to allow reuse but prevent commercial exploitation.

---

## 5. Creative Commons Zero 1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0)

**Identifier:** `CC0-1.0`

**Official License:** https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

**Description:**  
The creator waives all copyright and related rights to the extent allowed by law, placing the work in the public domain.

**Permissions:**

- ✅ Commercial use
- ✅ Modification
- ✅ Redistribution
- ✅ No attribution required

**Requirements:**

- None.

**Best For:**

- Users who want to dedicate their work to the public domain.

## Recommended Default

For user-uploaded images, the recommended default license is:

> **All Rights Reserved**

This ensures users retain full ownership and control unless they explicitly choose a more permissive license.
