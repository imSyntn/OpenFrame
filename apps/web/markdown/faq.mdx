# Frequently Asked Questions

---

## Is OpenFrame free to use?

Yes, OpenFrame is completely free to browse, download and upload photos.

## Can I use photos commercially?

Usage rights depend on the license selected by the uploader. Please review the license information provided on each photo before using it commercially.

## How do I upload photos?

Create an account and use the [Submit](/submit) page to upload your photos.

## What image formats are supported?

OpenFrame currently supports the following image formats:

- JPG
- JPEG
- PNG

## What is the maximum file size?

The maximum allowed file size is 10 MB per image.

## Do I retain ownership of my photos?

Yes. You retain full ownership and copyright of all photos you upload to OpenFrame.

## How do I report copyright infringement?

If you believe a photo infringes your copyright, please email us at [imsyntn@gmail.com](mailto:imsyntn@gmail.com) with:

- The URL of the image
- Proof that you own the copyright
- Your contact information

We will review your request and take appropriate action.

## Does OpenFrame verify copyright ownership?

No. OpenFrame is an open platform where users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to upload the content they submit.

## Does OpenFrame use tracking cookies?

No. OpenFrame does not use advertising or analytics cookies. Only essential cookies required for authentication and security are used.

## Do I need an account to download photos?

No. You can browse and download publicly available photos without creating an account.

## Can I delete my account and photos?

Yes. You may request account deletion at any time and all associated photos and data will be permanently removed.
